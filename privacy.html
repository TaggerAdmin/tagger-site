Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2025

TAGGER is designed with privacy as a core principle. We do not collect, store, process, share, or transmit any personal information or usage data of any kind.

Information Collection

TAGGER does not collect personal information, analytics, device identifiers, location data, or any other type of user data. All app functionality occurs entirely on the user’s device.

On-Device Processing

All operations within TAGGER take place locally on your device. No data is uploaded, transmitted, or sent to TAGGER developers or any external service. TAGGER has no technical ability to access or retrieve any information generated within the app.

Data Storage

No information is stored on external servers. Any data created within the app remains solely on the user’s device and is fully under the user’s control.

Third-Party Services

TAGGER does not integrate any analytics platforms, advertising networks, tracking tools, or third-party SDKs that could collect user information.

Children’s Privacy

Because TAGGER collects no data whatsoever, it is suitable for users of all ages, including children.

Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. If changes are made, the updated version will be posted on this page. We recommend reviewing this page periodically for any updates.
