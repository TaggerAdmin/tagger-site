Frequently Asked Questions

1. What is TAGGER?

Tagger is a painting app that allows you to put graffiti style art onto a number of pre-designed canvases. You can also import your own photos to use as canvases.

2. Does TAGGER store my photos?

Not directly, you can save or share your artwork to either your photos or send to friends through Whatsapp, Air Drop etc.

3. Does TAGGER collect any personal data?

No. It's a standalone app that is on your device and requires no server or user details to use.

4. Is TAGGER free to use?

After a small purchase fee - it's yours FOREVER! No annoying pop up ads, no in-app purchases. You wont be interrupted whilst you are creating masterpieces!

5. Will TAGGER receive new features or updates?

Yes. Your feedback is very important to us. We will continue to make the app better. Bug fixes, performance updates, new canvases and new features will be included in future updates.

6. Who do I contact for support?

Email us at taggeradmin@proton.me if you have any questions. There is a tutorial built into the app, and we will be putting detailed tutorial pages in this site as well. Or go to the Support page.

7. Does TAGGER work offline?

Absolutely! Since the app is just on your device, there is no need for internet access for the standard operation of the app. The only time you will need internet access is if you plan to share your artwork.
