Frequently Asked Questions

1. What is TAGGER?

Tagger is a painting app that allows you to put graffiti style art onto a number of pre-designed canvases. You can also import your own photos to use as canvases./p>

2. Does TAGGER store my photos?

Not directly, you can save or share your artwork to either your photos or send to friends through Whatsapp, Air Drop etc.

3. Does TAGGER collect any personal data?

No. Its a standalone app that is on your device and requires no server or user details to use./p>

4. Is TAGGER free to use?

After a small purchase fee - its yours FOREVER! No annoying pop up ads, no in-app purchases. You wont be interrupted whilst you are creating masterpieces!
